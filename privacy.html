Privacy Policy

Introduction

ContactHub values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how we help protect your data while you use our Contact Management System.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use ContactHub, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Numbers
  • Contact Details
  • Company Information
  • Addresses
  • Notes and Categories

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide the core functionality of ContactHub.

  • Authenticate users securely.
  • Store your contacts safely.
  • Allow Create, Read, Update and Delete (CRUD) operations.
  • Improve application performance and user experience.
  • Provide technical support when required.

3. Data Security

ContactHub uses Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore to securely manage user authentication and contact data. Although we use secure technologies, users should also create strong passwords and keep their login credentials confidential.

4. Your Rights

Every user has the right to:

  • View personal information.
  • Update profile details.
  • Edit stored contacts.
  • Delete contacts permanently.
  • Request account removal in future versions.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

ContactHub may use browser local storage to improve user experience. This may include:

  • Dark Mode preference.
  • Remembering login sessions handled by Firebase.
  • User interface preferences.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as ContactHub introduces new features and improvements. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.
